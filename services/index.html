Services

Complete retail fixture solutions

Three connected capabilities, delivered by one accountable team: the fixtures that hold your product, the fit-out that makes the space, and the branding that makes it yours.

1

Retail fixtures

Concept design and in-house production of high-quality crafted fixtures for every store format, finished to your brand specification.

  • Exclusive Brand Outlet (EBO) fixtures
  • Multiple Brand Outlet (MBO) fixtures
  • Shop in Shop (SIS) systems
  • Gondolas and free-standing display units
  • Solid wood, MDF, veneer and laminate fixtures
  • Metal fabrication in all forms and shapes
  • Acrylic, lighting and electrical structures
  • Custom-made specifications, finish to match
2

Store fit-outs

Complete turnkey interiors that take an empty shell to a finished, trading-ready store, on schedule.

  • Civil works
  • Interior build and finishing
  • MEP: mechanical, electrical and plumbing
  • Panelling and surface treatments
3

In-store branding

The finishing layer that makes a store unmistakably on-brand: lit, clean and consistent across every location.

  • Backlit frames and print
  • Specialised LED signage
  • ACP cladding
  • Store-front signage

Finishes

Surfaces and finishes we produce

Solid wood Veneer & laminate High-gloss paint finish Speciality coating Rusty powder coating Metal & acrylic Lighting & electrical

How we deliver

One team, from brief to handover

Brief & concept

We design fixtures and layouts from your brand guidelines, store format, budget and timeline.

Production

Installation

Handover
