How we work

From brief to handover

A clear, accountable process that keeps your rollout on schedule and on brand.

Brief & concept

We start from your brand guidelines, store format and timeline, then design fixtures and layouts that fit the space and the budget.

Production

Modules are manufactured in-house across wood, metal, acrylic and lighting, with finishing to the agreed specification.

Installation

Our site teams install on location anywhere in India, fitting modules with the right decorum at the store level.

Handover

We hand over a trading-ready store, finished, branded and lit, ready to open on schedule.
