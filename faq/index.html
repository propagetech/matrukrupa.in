FAQ

Questions, answered

What we make, the formats and materials we work with, where we install, and how to get started.

What does Matrukrupa Enterprise do?

We provide complete retail fixture solutions. That means three connected things: retail fixtures (the units that hold and display your product), complete store fit-outs (civil works, interiors, MEP and panelling), and in-store branding (backlit print, LED signage, ACP cladding and store-front signage). We design, manufacture and install all of it as one team.

What store formats do you build fixtures for?

We build for every common retail format: Exclusive Brand Outlets (EBO), Multiple Brand Outlets (MBO), Shop in Shop (SIS) and gondola or free-standing display systems. We specialise in delivering small and mid-sized retail formats to perfection.

Which materials and finishes do you work with?

We process fixtures in solid wood, MDF, veneer, laminates, painted and coated surfaces, metal in all forms and shapes, acrylic, and lighting and electrical structures, all to custom-made specifications. Finishes include high-gloss paint, speciality coatings and rusty powder coating.

Do you install across India?

Yes. We install on a pan-India basis. Our site teams fit modules on location anywhere in the country, bringing out the right decorum at the store level, and we implement brand specifications consistently across stores.

Can you work to our brand guidelines?

Absolutely. We understand and implement brand specifications across stores, adhering to expected brand guidelines and standards. Working to brand guidelines is part of how we deliver every project.

Which retail sectors do you serve?

Our engineers and management team have experience across retail verticals including fashion, lifestyle, footwear, clothing, food and cosmetics, and more. We have worked with leading architects and large Indian and multinational retailers.

How experienced is the team?

Matrukrupa Enterprise was formed in 2008 by professionals with real factory-floor experience in retail fixtures and fit-outs. Today we can proudly claim more than 100 man-years of combined experience in concept design and production of high-quality crafted fixtures.

How do we get a quote?

Email us at info@matrukrupa.in or use the contact form, and tell us your brand, the type of retail you are in, how many outlets you have and your timeline. We will come back with how we can design, manufacture and install your store. Nothing you enter on this site is stored on the server.
