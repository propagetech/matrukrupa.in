ABOUT US

When hard core professionals with factory floor experience in the business of retail fixtures & fit outs came together . . . . . . . . . the formation of 'Matrukrupa Enterprise' occurred in the year 2008!

Yes, it is true that today we can proudly claim of having more than '100 man years of experience' in Concept Designing and Production of high qualitative crafted Fixtures.

While the growth of the small and mid-sized retail segments in India are becoming increasingly popular we specialise in delivering these formats to perfection.

Our work culture is a blend of man and machine built on the strength of professionally trained personnel ensuring perfection in our production & product.

Our skill lies in finishing the modules with appropriate installation styles bringing out the right decorum at the store site level.

We also understand and implement Brand specifications across stores on a Pan India basis adhering to expected Brand Guidelines & Standards.

As we are passionate about our work, we are always willing to include innovative and tech savvy configurations in our projects.

Our site engineers and management team has experience across verticals in retails like fashion, life styles, footwear, clothing, food, cosmetics and so on providing value added service on all quarters.

With expertise in various areas of attractive surface displays, utility directed strategies and user friendly themes, one can bank upon us with reliability for Timely & Qualitative products.

Today, we have the ability to process fixtures made in Solid wood, MDF, veneer, laminates, painted surface, metal in all forms and shape, acrylic, lighting and electrical structures with custom made specifications.

Having worked with the best of architects and large corporations (Indian and multinational) over a period of time, we have built a reputation of developing excellence in our work. We have been rated as one of the trend setters in interior designing of compact and clear out lays at competitive rates.
