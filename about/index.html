About us

Retail fixtures, built by people who know the factory floor

Matrukrupa Enterprise was formed in 2008 when hard-core professionals with real factory-floor experience in retail fixtures and fit-outs came together to build complete store environments, the right way.

Our story

Formed in 2008, grown on detail

When hard-core professionals with factory-floor experience in retail fixtures and fit-outs came together, Matrukrupa Enterprise was formed in 2008. Today we can proudly claim more than 100 man-years of experience in concept design and production of high-quality crafted fixtures.

While the small and mid-sized retail segments in India grow increasingly popular, we specialise in delivering these formats to perfection. Our work culture is a blend of man and machine, built on the strength of professionally trained personnel ensuring quality in our production and product.

Having worked with the best of architects and large corporations, Indian and multinational, we have built a reputation for excellence and have been rated among the trend-setters in interior designing of compact, clear layouts at competitive rates.

Our vision

World-class fixtures, on time, at the store

Our objective is to provide world-class complete retail fixture solutions within the required timeline at the store level, and to be one of the most preferred service providers in the country for the complete range of retail fixture solutions.

Our quality

The difference is in the detail

Our single-minded belief lies in a simple proposition: the difference is in the detail. This belief translates into action in many ways, as we continuously set new standards through professionalism, technological inclusions and speed of work.

What we work with

Materials and finishes, with custom specifications

We have the ability to process fixtures across a wide range of materials, all to custom-made specifications.

  • Solid wood
  • MDF
  • Veneer and laminates
  • Painted and coated surfaces
  • Metal in all forms and shapes
  • Acrylic
  • Lighting and electrical structures
  • High-gloss, speciality and rusty-powder coatings

Reach

Pan-India, to brand standards

We understand and implement brand specifications across stores on a pan-India basis, adhering to expected brand guidelines and standards.

Our site engineers and management team have experience across retail verticals: fashion, lifestyle, footwear, clothing, food, cosmetics and more, providing value-added service on all fronts. Our skill lies in finishing modules with the right installation styles, bringing out the right decorum at the store.
